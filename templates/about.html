How SpeedCubeMuse works

What is SpeedCubeMuse?

SpeedCubeMuse is an AI-powered tool that lets you query World Cube Association competition data using plain English. Instead of writing SQL, just ask a question and get instant results.

How It Works

1

Ask a Question

Type any question about WCA data in natural language.

2

AI Translation

Anthropic's Claude AI translates your question into a SQL query against the WCA database.

3

Get Results

The query runs against the WCA database and results are displayed in a formatted table.

Database

The app queries a database populated from the official WCA data export (March 14, 2026), containing:

281,645 Competitors
6,346,883 Results
17,150 Competitions
21 Events

Example Questions

  • What is the world record for 3x3?
  • Who are the top 10 fastest 2x2 solvers?
  • How many competitions have been held in the United States in 2025?
  • Who has the most world record single results?
  • Who is the top 3x3 competitor with the first name "Timothy"?
  • Who placed first in 3x3 finals at the 2023 World Championship?

Tips for Better Results

  • Be Specific

    Make sure to be as precise as possible when formulating your question so the AI knows exactly what you're looking for, i.e., specify between single and average results.

  • Use Clear Language

    Avoid ambiguous terms and use clear, unambiguous language when asking your question, i.e., use "United States" instead of "America".

  • Understand how WCA Results are Structured

    Having a basic understanding of WCA results and competition data can help you ask more effective questions and interpret the results.

  • Experiment with Different Phrasings

    Try rephrasing your question in different ways to see if you get more accurate or detailed results.. i.e, ask "Who placed first in 3x3 finals" instead of asking "who won the competition".

Ask a Delegate

Ask a Delegate is an AI-powered chatbot trained on the full WCA Regulations and Guidelines. You can ask it anything you'd normally ask a WCA Delegate at a competition — rules clarifications, procedural questions, penalty interpretations, and more.

How It Works

1

Ask Your Question

Type any question about WCA rules, procedures, or competition logistics in plain English.

2

Semantic Search

Your question is matched against all 697 WCA regulations and guidelines using vector similarity search (Voyage AI embeddings + pgvector), then re-ranked to surface the most relevant passages.

3

Grounded Answer

Claude generates a response grounded strictly in the retrieved regulations, with citations linked directly to the official WCA Regulations page.

Example Questions

  • What happens if a competitor touches the puzzle after stopping the timer?
  • Can a competitor use a different cube than the one assigned to them?
  • What is the correct procedure for a +2 penalty?
  • How many attempts does a competitor get for 3x3 blindfolded?
  • What counts as a DNF in fewest moves?

Guest Access

Guests can ask up to 5 questions without signing in. Sign in with Google or your WCA account for unlimited access and conversation history.

Discord Bot

SpeedCubeMuse is also available as a Discord bot, bringing the same natural language query capabilities directly into your server!

Commands

!wca query <question> Ask a question about WCA data. Aliases: !wca q, !wca ask
!wca help Show available commands and example queries
!wca ping Check bot latency

Add to Your Server

1

Click the Invite Link

Use this link to add SpeedCubeMuse to your Discord server.

2

Select Your Server

Choose the server you want to add the bot to from the dropdown. You must have Manage Server permissions in that server.

3

Authorize Permissions

Review and approve the requested permissions, then click Authorize. The bot only needs permission to send messages.

4

Start Querying

Head to any text channel and type !wca query followed by your question. For example: !wca q Who has the fastest 3x3 single?
